Base64 Converter

Base64 Encoder & Decoder

Base64 is a group of binary-to-text encoding schemes. It is commonly used to encode data that needs to be stored and transferred over media that are designed to deal with textual data.

Encode: Converts plain text into Base64 string.

Decode: Converts Base64 string back into plain text.
